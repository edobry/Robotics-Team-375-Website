Team 375's Animation Team works during the season and offseason on 30 second 3D Animations using programs provided by Autodesk. We use 3ds Max, Combustion and many other programs to create these animation. Each year in the preseason, we get a Safety Animation topic and make a 30 second animation that enforces the safety teachings of FIRST. During the season, we get the Autodesk Visualization Contest topic that ranges from environmental issues, inventions to help society, and this year, Biomimicry.

    The Animation Team writes, models, animates and edits the 30 second animations submited each year for the AVA (Autodesk Visualization Award) and Safety Animation, despite having only three members.

    We work in the same room as the website team, giving suggestions when they need them, and go down to the lab to lend a hand to the build team.

      With only three members and little mentor guidance, it is a challenge to us creating a complex 30-second animation. To make up for the few members, we usually stay pretty late, sometimes till 8:30. We work hard all season to get the animation done and love the new challenge we get every year from FIRST.
