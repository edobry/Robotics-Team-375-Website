So, what does the programming team actually do? For starters, we program (if the title of the group did not give that away already). To sum it all up, our task is to make the controllers talk to the robot, but, unfortunately, the robot is not too social at times; however, that is why we are here! We use programs such as Windriver and Labview to assign different tasks to pretty much anything that functions. Joysticks, switches, buttons - you name it. We also make sure that the robot can function autonomously during the first 15-second period of the game.

    It programming difficult? Sure. Is it rewarding? You bet!
