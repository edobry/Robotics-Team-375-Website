The Writing Team at 375 is responsible for composing the Woodie Flowers and Chairman's Award submissions. They compile all the photos and videos taken of the team over the years, decide what volunteer activities the team will partake in, work with mentors on scheduling upcoming media coverage, and write the letters the team sends out and the awards we submit.
   
    The Writing Team handles all requirements for the essay submissions including:
        1. creating and editing the Chairman's video
        2. writing the Chairman's script for both the video and the judging session
        3. gathering all the necessary materials (letters of recommendation, poster boards, etc.) needed for the Chairman's presentation
        4. making up the team's business plan
        5. getting quotes for the Woodie Flowers Award
        6. selecting the pictures that go in the WF and Chairman's award
        7. tallying up the team votes of which mentor we enter for WF
        8. contacting alumni for fact-check information
